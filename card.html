Lesser Short-Nosed Fruit Bat

This ubercute fruit bat can live for up to 30 years!

  • Scientific name: Cynopterus brachyotis
  • Family: Pteropodidae
  • Habitat: Disturbed forest, mangroves
  • Cuteness index: 9999

Lesser short-nosed fruit bat is a smaller member of the impressive megabat family residing in South and SE Asia and Indonesia. To match their sweet looks, these little fellas feed on tasty aromatic fruit, such as mangoes, or flower nectar.
